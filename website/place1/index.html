OUR ACHIEVEMENTS

The students are also motivated and equipped to participate in the Campus placement programs.
Renowned companies with attractive salary packages are invited for the campus recruitment drive.
Companies which are part of on-campus placements are from Software and Core Engineering industry.

FUNCTIONS OF PLACEMENT ACTIVITIES

To Organize On / Off campus Interview for the final year students
To Promote for Industry Institute Interface activities.
To Arrange Career / Personal Counselling sessions.
To Organize Career Guidance sessions and Personality development programs.
To Organize Functional Skill Development Program.
To Organize Placement training Programs like Aptitude programs,
Life skills programs, Motivational sessions, Resume Writing,
Group discussion, Mock Interview.
